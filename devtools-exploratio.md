## Website 3: https://www.youtube.com

1. **5 Different HTML Elements Identified**:
   - `<div>` — Used for main containers and video cards
   - `<img>` — Used for video thumbnails
   - `<h3>` — Used for video titles
   - `<a>` — Used for links to videos and channels
   - `<button>` — Used for search, like, and menu buttons

2. **Form Element Found**:
   - The search bar at the top is a `<form>` element.
   - Inputs inside the form:
     - `<input type="text">` (search input field)
     - `<button>` (search button)
